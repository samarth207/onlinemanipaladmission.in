Semester 1

Principles of Management and Organizational Behavior

This subject helps learners connect management theory with real-world practice, building practical skills in planning, organizing, and controlling across diverse work settings. It also equips learners to understand organizational behavior, structure, culture, and change to manage people and teams effectively in today’s workplaces.

Marketing Management

In this subject, learners will understand core marketing concepts, consumer behavior, and research methods to develop and analyze effective marketing strategies. They will also learn to apply principles of segmentation, pricing, promotion, distribution, and digital marketing to evaluate market performance.

Accounting for Managers

In this subject, learners will gain a solid foundation in financial accounting, including maintaining books of accounts and preparing financial statements. They will also learn to analyze a company’s financial performance using ratios, cost sheets, and other key financial techniques.

Business Economics

In this subject, learners will understand how microeconomic concepts and techniques guide business decision-making and relate to areas like marketing, finance, and production. They will also learn to apply these tools to forecast demand, calculate costs, pricing, and profits, and solve real-world business problems.

Business Communication

In this subject, learners will develop essential corporate communication skills, both written and oral, for effective workplace interactions. They will also learn to craft business letters, reports, press releases, advertising copy, and professional CVs with cover letters.

Legal Aspects of Business

In this subject, learners will understand the fundamentals of business law, including contract formation, legal obligations, and consequences of business transactions. They will also learn to apply laws like the Sale of Goods Act and Consumer Protection Act, and develop skills to analyze and present legal arguments effectively.

Computer Application in Management

In this subject, learners will understand how computers and IT support business operations, communication, and decision-making. They will also learn about hardware, software, programming, databases, and networks to effectively store, retrieve, and utilize business data.
Semester 2

Quantitative Methods in Management

In this subject, learners will gain foundational knowledge of statistics and its applications in business, including probability, distributions, and data visualization. They will also learn techniques like linear programming, Markov processes, and queuing theory to support decision-making and problem-solving.

Human Resource Management

In this subject, learners will understand the key concepts, functions, and practices of human resource management. They will also develop practical skills to manage employees, including creating job descriptions, performance appraisals, induction plans, and incentive structures in modern organizations.

Financial Management

In this subject, learners will understand the roles and responsibilities of modern finance executives in corporate settings. They will also develop skills to analyze financial statements, apply concepts like time value of money, capital budgeting, capital structure, dividend decisions, and taxation to make informed financial decisions.

Productions and Operations Management

In this subject, learners will understand operations management in manufacturing and service sectors, including planning, production, and service decisions. They will also learn to manage inventory, design plant layouts, prepare production schedules, and ensure quality for efficient industrial operations.

Research Methodology and Statistical Techniques

In this subject, learners will understand the fundamentals of business research, including design, data collection, and analysis techniques. They will also develop skills in sampling, statistical analysis, data presentation, and preparing clear, effective research reports.

Global Economic Environment and Policy

In this subject, learners will understand key macroeconomic concepts, including national income, credit systems, trade policies, and economic systems, and their impact on business decisions. They will also explore the relationship between society and business to support sound and responsible business practices.

Management Information Systems

In this subject, learners will understand the role of Management Information Systems (MIS) in supporting business strategy and processes. They will also learn to manage organizational data effectively and leverage business intelligence to improve managerial decision-making.
Semester 3

Core Subjects

Project Management

This subject equips learners with a strategic and practical understanding of project management principles, tools, and techniques. It focuses on planning, execution, risk management, and performance evaluation to successfully deliver projects aligned with organizational goals.

Business Strategy

This subject offers a comprehensive understanding of the business environment and the core principles of strategic management. It develops learners’ ability to analyze competitive scenarios and implement corporate and business-level strategies using structured frameworks and case studies.

Eligibility for online MBA

Educational qualification

Candidates must have completed 10+2+3-year or 10+2+4-year or 10+3+3-year bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university/institution, or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) or other competent bodies.

Grades

Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate in graduation (45% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category).

What is the eligibility criteria for an Online MBA course? Who can apply?

Educational qualification   

Candidates must have completed 10+2+3-year or 10+2+4-year or 10+3+3-year bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university/institution, or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) or other competent bodies. 

Candidates who have completed their 10+2 or graduate level education outside India must produce a certificate of equivalence issued by the Association of Indian Universities. 

Grades  

Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate at graduation.  

Other requirements   

Candidates who are not Indian citizens or residing outside India and holding NRE or PIO status will be billed an international fee for respective degrees & may need to submit documents mentioned but not limited to CV, address proof, valid visa, PR card & passport copy as part of the application process. 

What is the duration of an MBA program?

Like an on-campus MBA, the online MBA from Sikkim Manipal University is a two-year degree program consisting of four semesters. However, students can choose to complete the degree at their own pace in a maximum of 4 years.

What is the admission process for an online MBA degree?

Here’s a guide to help you begin your online MBA program at Sikkim Manipal University:

Step 1: Fill in your basic, education, & work experience-related details and pay the application fee to register.
Step 2: Pay the admission fee for the first semester/year or full program.
Step 3: Upload supporting documents and submit your application.
Step 4: The university will evaluate your documents to confirm your admission.

*What are the terms & conditions for EMIs and other easy financing options? 

*Zero cost EMI option is available only on payment of annual fee.

The EMI amount mentioned above applies to the annual fee and must be paid monthly for 12 months.

Approval of loans from our loan partners depends on applicants meeting credit norms and submitting required documents. Loan partners may impose other fees and may have additional terms & conditions that must be met by applicants.

For further details on EMIs and other easy financing options, please contact our admission counsellors.

More FAQs

Can students take online examinations from any device?

Students can take up online examinations from any desktop or laptop running a Windows Operating System with the latest version of the Google Chrome browser installed. The Safe Exam Browser (exam portal) is not compatible with iOS devices.

What is the evaluation & assessment pattern? 

  • A student’s performance evaluation will be based on both continuous assessments (CA) through assignments and term-end examinations (TEE) which consist of descriptive and multiple-choice questions. A student must score at least 40% in each of these to be declared as having passed the exam. The assessment ratio of TEE to CA is 70:30.
  • A student must score at least 40% in both the parts to be declared as having passed.

What is the examination pattern for online degrees at Sikkim Manipal University? 

  • The University term-end examination (TEE) will be held digitally viz. computer-based and online proctored.
  • Candidates can appear for the exams from the comfort of their homes.
  • The details of the examination schedule will be made available in the photo admit card. Other details on the examination pattern are available in the program guide provided to students.
  • Examination results will be published on the student portal. If a student fails to clear any of the papers (either in UE or IA or both), he must re-appear by applying to the University through the re-sitting application along with relevant fees, within the due date.
  • On satisfying all criteria stipulated by the University, successful students will be awarded their respective program degree by the University.

What is the minimum grade to complete the online degree program? 

Students must score at least 40% in continuous evaluation and 40% in term-end examinations in each course to successfully complete the program. In case the minimum score is not attained, they will need to apply for re-sit with applicable fees and appear for exam to pass the course.

More FAQs

How are Sikkim Manipal University’s online degree programs delivered? 

Sikkim Manipal University’s online programs are delivered in a 100% online mode so that students can attend live classes and complete the program remotely, while also pursuing their professional and personal commitments in parallel.

Will the program have live lectures and webinars? 

Yes. The program is delivered through a mix of live lectures, pre- recorded videos and discussion forums in which students can interact directly with faculty and self-paced learning. Self-paced learning is driven by video lectures, e-books, quizzes, and assignments that are delivered using a state-of-the-art Learning Management System (LMS).

Are there any mandatory sessions? 

Students are expected to attend live classes, watch recorded sessions, and go through at least 75% of the self-learning content (reading material and video lectures) available on LMS. This would ensure they are eligible to appear for the final exams.

When will I get access to the learning modules? 

After admission is confirmed by the university, students will receive access to the Learning Management System (LMS), student portal and e-library. Students can then start accessing the course material and begin their learning journey.

What are the study materials given to learners of online degree programs at Sikkim Manipal University? 

Students are given access to an exhaustive e-library, video lectures, audio-visual interactive material, virtual classroom sessions, audio podcasts, virtual simulations, and self-assessment quizzes or tests. Students are free to download e-books and print them for convenience.

What is dual specialization in an MBA program?

Dual specialization allows students to pursue two areas of expertise within a single MBA program. This helps broaden career opportunities by allowing students to gain in-depth knowledge and skills in two complementary domains.

How does dual specialization work in the online MBA program at SMU?

During the MBA program, learners can choose two specialization areas in semester 3, and continue the same in semester 4. These are pursued in parallel during the program, allowing students to graduate with competencies in both domains.

Can I pursue online MBA from Sikkim Manipal University with a single specialization?

No. The MBA program from SMU is offered with a dual specialization. The first specialization is marketing, which is compulsory for all learners. For the second specialization, learners can choose from four options i.e., Finance, HR, Systems, and Operations & Supply Chain Management.

How can I get my academic queries answered? 

Students will be assigned dedicated course mentors and coordinators who can be contacted to clarify doubts using discussion forums, instant messaging, and email on the Learning Management System (LMS).

How much time should I dedicate to complete the learning activities? 

Although the learning is flexible, students are expected to spend at least 15-20 hours per week watching concept videos, reading learning material, attempting quizzes, and attending live webinars. This would ensure that you get your degree within the minimum duration.

How is an MBA with dual specialization different from a regular MBA?

Here’s a quick comparison to help your understanding of the differences between online MBA courses and regular MBA programs: 

Aspect   Online MBA (Dual specialization)  Regular MBA  
Learning Mode   Online, with live + recorded sessions   In-person, campus-based  
Specialization Focus  Offers in-depth learning in two domains simultaneously  Focuses on a single domain (e.g., Finance or Marketing) 
Flexibility   High – Learn anytime, anywhere   Fixed schedule and classroom timings  
Career Continuity   Allows you to work while studying   Typically requires a career break  
Pace of Learning   Self-paced, adaptable to work-life balance   Structured and time-bound  
Cost & Convenience   More affordable, no relocation needed   Higher costs including relocation  

Who should opt for MBA with dual specialization?

The dual specialization online MBA program of Sikkim Manipal University provides students with the option of choosing from the fields of Finance, HR, Marketing, Systems, and Operations & Supply Chain Management. It is perfect for those who want to diversify their career with the flexibility of dual specialization.

What is the most popular dual specialization in MBA?

Through the online MBA program of Sikkim Manipal University, learners are given an opportunity to select a dual specialization according to their needs. They can opt for two of the following: Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, Systems, Healthcare, or Operations & Supply Chain Management to get a wide range of domain knowledge.

Why choose an online MBA degree from Manipal universities?

The online MBA program with dual specialization from Sikkim Manipal University (SMU), an institution with NAAC A+ accreditation, provides professionals with necessary business management knowledge. Students have the freedom to tailor their MBA by picking any two specializations from Marketing, Finance, HR, Systems, Operations & Supply Chain Management, or Healthcare, thus becoming multi-skilled professionals and gaining the ability to face the challenges of the rapidly changing business world. All the online degree programs are UGC entitled and compliant with AICTE norms.
