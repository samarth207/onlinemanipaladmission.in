Advantages of Our Business Analytics Courses

Upon graduating from the online master’s in business analytics course, online learners get a certificate that confirms their skills and expertise in business analytics. This certification is accredited by top organizations globally, boosting your reputation and career potential across the worldwide employment sector. 

Data-Driven Decision-Making Focus

Our online Business Analytics course highlights the hands-on application of analytics in real business scenarios. Learn to obtain strategic findings from data and make well-grounded decisions that foster organizational success and long-term objectives.   

Continuous Assessment & Feedback

Stay on course with your milestones through ongoing assignments, quizzes, and assessments in our online business analytics courses. Constructive feedback from faculty helps you identify strengths and performance gaps, encouraging sustained growth and skill enhancement.

Personalized Learning Support

Our focus-driven guidance team makes sure that every individual gets customized support over the duration of the course. From academic mentoring to resolving queries, we provide a learning mentorship that aligns students’ learning requirements and objectives.

    Learn More About Business Analytics Courses (FAQs)

    What is Business Analytics?

    Business Analytics is the domain of using data, statistical models, and technology to identify trends, shape growth strategies, and inspire data-centric decisions. It merges industry expertise with statistical analysis to enhance workflow optimization and revenue growth.  

    How can I access the learning materials for this course?

    All the learning materials are accessible digitally through the Learning Management System (LMS). You can use them at your convenience on your desktop or mobile device. 

    Are online courses by Online Manipal credible and valid?

    Yes, online courses offered at Online Manipal are UGC entitled and tailored for industry requirements, equipping you with theoretical knowledge and practical skills that employers seek, besides being job ready. 

    More FAQs

    What is the payment refund policy for these courses?

    MAHE follows UGC regulations for refund of course fee. 

    • 15 days or more prior to admissions closing date – 100% refund 
    • Within 15 days prior to the admission closing date – 90% refund 
    • Within 15 days after the admission closing date – 80% refund 
    • 15 days after the admission closing date – 50% Refund 
    • 30 days after the admission closing date – No Refund 

    MUJ Follows UGC regulations for refund of courses fee. 

    • 15 days or more prior to the admission closing date – 100% refund* 
    • Less than 15 days before the admission closing date – 90% refund 
    • Within 15 days after the admission closing date – 80% refund 
    • More than 15 days but within 30 days after the admission closing date – 50% refund 
    • More than 30 days after the admission closing date – No refund

    More FAQs

    What does a Business Analytics expert do?

    A Business Analytics professional evaluates data to observe shifts, patterns, and possibilities that aid strategic business decisions. They analyze complex datasets, develop predictive models, design dashboards, and report insights that drive breakthroughs and evolutions. 

    More FAQs

    Are these online Business Analytics courses for freshers or experienced professionals?

    These business analytics programs are relevant for both freshers and employed individuals. The course content is crafted to start with the fundamentals and eventually moves to advanced topics, making it suitable for learners from diverse backgrounds. 

    More FAQs

    What are the top skills to become a Business Analytics expert?

    Key skills to become a Business Analytics expert include: 

    • Data analysis and visualization 
    • Statistical modeling 
    • Business intelligence 
    • SQL and Excel proficiency 
    • Python or R programming 
    • Critical thinking 
    • Decision-making and communication 

    What tools and skills do we learn from these Business Analytics courses?

    You’ll gain hands-on experience with tools such as: 

    • Excel, SQL, Tableau/Power BI 
    • Python or R for analytics 
    • Predictive modeling techniques
    • Data storytelling 
    • Business intelligence platforms 

    How are the classes conducted?

    Classes are conducted through live sessions on weekends. Recorded videos will be available for learners on the LMS. Students can also interact in conversation in the discussion forum.
