Learn More About Our MSc Programs (FAQs)

What is a Master of Science (MSc) Degree?

An MSc is a post-graduate degree tailored for science, technology or analytical subjects which are aimed at deepening knowledge and improving job prospects in specialized areas.

Why choose online MSc course from Manipal universities?

Online MSc programs from Manipal University Jaipur are UGC-entitled, industry-aligned, and offer the flexibility to learn from anywhere with expert faculty support.

How can I access the learning materials for this course?

Learning materials like video lectures, electronic books, and quizzes can be accessed anytime and anywhere on the Learning Management System (LMS).

Will I get a degree after completing this program?

Yes, all eligible students who have successfully fulfilled every criterion will be awarded their degree certificates via in-person convocation on the campus or mailed via courier.

How are online MSc degree programs different from regular MSc courses?

Here’s a quick rundown to give you an idea of the difference between online MSc degree programs and regular on-campus MSc courses: 

Feature  Online MSc Degree Programs  Regular MSc Courses 
Mode of Learning  Fully online, self-paced or scheduled live sessions  In-person classroom learning 
Flexibility  High – learn anytime, anywhere  Fixed schedule and location 
Accessibility  Accessible globally with internet  Requires physical presence at the campus 
Interaction with Faculty  Through live sessions, emails, and discussion boards  Face-to-face interaction 
Learning Materials  Digital content, recorded lectures, e-books  Physical books, classroom notes 
Peer Interaction  Virtual discussions and forums  In-person classroom interactions 
Suitability  Ideal for working professionals and remote learners  Suitable for full-time students 
Degree Validity  UGC-entitled, equivalent to regular degrees  UGC-approved and widely recognized 
Cost  Generally, more affordable  May involve higher tuition and living expenses 

Are flexible payment options available for these courses? What is the eligibility criteria?

Yes, definitely flexible no-cost EMI and installment options are there. For more information about EMIs and other simple financing solutions, get in touch with our admission counsellors. 

Is a scholarship available for these courses? What is the eligibility criteria?

Yes. Scholarships are provided to the defense personnel, differently abled persons, and meritorious students 

  • 20% for defense personnel  
  • 20% for people with disabilities 
  • 15% For UG/PG Alumni of MAHE & AGE colleges with ≥ 75% (aggregate) at graduation 
  • 10% for meritorious students with ≥ 75% marks 
  • For international students/NRIs (Only Merit-based scholarships) 

Can an online MSc degree get you a job?

Yes, the degree is definitely recognized by employers, and you also receive placement support to find job opportunities in the industries that match your skill sets. 

What is the eligibility criteria for an Online MSc degree course? Who can apply?

To apply for an online MSc degree, candidates are required to possess a bachelor’s degree from a recognized university with a minimum percentage of marks. It is important to note that the eligibility criteria for different specializations may differ slightly. The requirement to be eligible is open to fresh graduates as well as those who are currently employed. 

What is the duration of the MSc program?

The duration of an Online MSc program is 2 years, and maximum is 4 years.

What is the admission process for an online MSc degree?

After choosing the program that suits you best, you can submit an application online at www.mahe.onlinemanipal.com. 

After you provide some basic information, you can pay your application fee of INR 1500 (non-refundable) and enter necessary education and work experience details. Make a payment for the admission fee covering either one semester/year or the total program fee. 

If you want, you can also call us at +91-7330 300 600 or send an email to info@mahe.onlinemanipal.com, and we will be there for you during the application process. 

Your admission process will be done once you upload the documents that are relevant. After the university approves them, your admission process will be complete.

Who should opt for online MSc courses?

These programs are ideal for working professionals, fresh graduates, or anyone who desires to upskill or change their career path in the fields of science or analytics.

From which universities will I get a Master of Science degree?

You will get a Master of Science (MSc) from Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), a NAAC A++ accredited institution that is UGC recognized and known for its academic excellence.

What are the required documents for admission into MSc course?

The documents that are necessary to get admission in the online program of Manipal Academy of Higher Education are: 

  • Proof of identity/address

Other documents

  • Work experience certificate 
  • CV 

Is it mandatory for all online students to have an Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) account?

Yes. From the academic year 2022-23, it has been made mandatory for all universities offering courses in online or distance learning modes to create ABC accounts for all their students as per the UGC regulation and the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. 

Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) is a digital storehouse of the credits earned by students during their academic journey. It is a credit bank for students that allows them to accumulate, verify, transfer, and redeem their credits. This is an initiative from the Ministry of Education, Government of India to enable academic flexibility and students’ mobility across multiple Higher Education Institutes (HEIs).  The University Grants Commission (UGC) has mandated that every learner who is an Indian national needs to submit the following details for creating their ABC account: 

  • Roll number issued by the university 
  • Aadhar number (12 digits) 
  • Name (as mentioned in Aadhaar) 
  • Gender 
  • Date of Birth 
  • Mobile number (linked to their Aadhaar) 

The university will reach out to enrolled students for details required to create their ABC accounts and will provide these details to UGC. Once the ABC account is created, the credits earned by the learners in their term-end exam will get deposited into their respective ABC accounts which they can redeem within 7 years. Students can email helpdesk@manipalacademyonline.edu.in or call +91-9845 818 965 for any queries.
