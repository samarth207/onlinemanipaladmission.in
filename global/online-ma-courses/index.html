Advantages of Our OnlineMA Courses

Flexibility to Learn at Your Own Pace

Whether you are a working professional or a full-time learner, an online MA will provide a remote setting with pre-recorded classes, and academic resources that offer flexibility. This will help students to easily include their classes into their daily routine.

Career Advancement Opportunities

Individuals or professionals seeking a promotion, preparing for a leadership role, or looking to transition to a new industry can choose a specialization and work across various sectors, including education, media, public policy, social work, or the arts.

Enhanced Research and Critical Thinking Skills

Online MA courses do not just give you a degree but also provide a space for deeper academic engagement. With its research-centric course structure, learners can evaluate sources critically, and present ideas in a structured, persuasive manner.

Global Networking and Exposure

With an online MA degree, learners get the opportunity to exchange ideas, gain fresh perspectives, and build meaningful professional relationships across borders. This paves the way in building cultural intelligence and expands your network for future collaborations.

Access to Cutting-Edge Digital Learning Tools

With access to digital libraries, discussion panels, academic databases, and so on, learners can enhance their learning journey with a personalized touch. Furthermore, these tools help improve engagement, making the learning process more dynamic.

Learn More About Our MA Programs (FAQs)

What are the different specializations available under MA?

Learners can select their specialization based on where their interests lie. Below are the specializations available under MA:  

  • English 
  • Political Science 
  • Sociology 
  • Economics 

Does online MA have value?

Just like any other offline university or college, an online MA holds the same value, especially when accredited by the UGC and accepted by employers. With a course structure of intense academic depth, it has the same relevance as any on-campus MA programs.   

Who should pursue an Online MA Course?

An online MA is ideal for: 

  • Working professionals looking forward to upskilling, change domains, or aiming for leadership roles 
  • Counselors, teachers, or social workers who want to advance academically 
  • Individuals who are planning to enter research programs  
  • Civil service aspirants  
  • Students who are looking for an online learning mode are encouraged 
  • Graduates from arts, humanities, or social science backgrounds 

What is the eligibility and course duration for the online MA?

  • Eligibility: Candidates must have completed 10+2+3-year bachelor’s degree from a recognized university/institution, or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities. 
  • Course Duration: An online MA takes 2 years to complete. But students are given the opportunity to finish it in 4 years.

What are the best career options after completing an MA course?

Some of the best career options that learners can apply to are as follows:  

  • Content Writer / Editor / Journalist 
  • Research Analyst 
  • Teacher / Lecturer / Academic Consultant 
  • Human Resource Executive 
  • Policy Analyst / Public Relations Officer 
  • Social Media Manager / Communication Specialist 
  • NGO / Development Sector Professional 
  • Psychologist / Counsellor (if MA in Psychology) 
  • Civil Services or other Competitive Exams Aspirant 

Graduates can also pursue further studies like MPhil or PhD to enter academia or research roles. 

Will I earn a degree from an accredited university?

Students can earn an online MA degree if they are enrolled in a UGC-recognized and NAAC-accredited university. These degrees are fully recognized, ensuring academic credibility.

What is the average salary after completing the MA course?

The salary of graduates after completing an MA differs on the basis of different factors such as location, experience, specialization, and role. But below is the average salary metrics 

  • Fresh graduates can look out for a package on the salary scale of 3-5 LPA 
  • Working professionals with around 3-5 years of experience can expect a salary range of 6-10 LPA.   
  • Those in research, policy-making, or creative industries can see steady growth with specialization and experience.
