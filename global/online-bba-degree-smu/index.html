Online BBA Program Benefits

Gateway to Advanced Studies

With a BBA from a university like SMU, learners gain a strong foundation for higher studies, including MBA programs and the intended specializations. This will help students to prepare for academic growth and a long-term career.

Flexible & Self-Paced Learning

Upon enrolling in an online program, learners can study at their own pace and access e-learning materials, assignments, and other resources from anywhere. The flexibility factor helps them steer work, internships, and family commitments without compromising on education.

Entrepreneurial Advantage

Learners can build essential skills to start, manage, and expand their businesses. Further, with strategic planning, better financial management, and market analysis, learners can apply their knowledge in real-world scenarios, helping them launch their ventures.

Robust alumni network

Establish valuable connections with professionals from a wide range of backgrounds and gain industry insights that can be invaluable while navigating the competitive job market. Get access to Sikkim Manipal University’s prestigious alumni network.

Fully online experience with campus immersion

Benefit from a 100% online experience with zero compromise on quality through online enrollment, orientation, classes, assignments, projects, examinations, and placement. Get assistance from dedicated course mentors and 24×7 student support. Also, participate in exclusive campus events and interact with peers & faculty.

Eligibility for online BBA

Educational qualification

Candidates must have completed 10+2 from a recognized national or state board institution or 10+3 diploma from a recognized national or state institute or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities in any discipline.

Educational qualification

Candidates must have completed 10+2 from a recognized national or state board institution or 10+3 diploma from a recognized national or state institute or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities in any discipline.

Candidates who have completed 10+2 education outside India must produce a certificate of equivalence issued by the Association of Indian Universities.

Other requirements

Candidates who are not Indian citizens or residing outside India and holding NRE or PIO status will be billed an international fee for respective degrees & may need to submit documents mentioned but not limited to CV, address proof, valid visa, PR card & passport copy as part of the application process.

Is it mandatory for all online students to have an Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) account?

Yes. From the academic year 2022-23, it has been made mandatory for all universities offering courses in online or distance learning modes to create ABC accounts for all their students as per the UGC regulation and the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. 

Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) is a digital storehouse of the credits earned by students during their academic journey. It is a credit bank for students that allows them to accumulate, verify, transfer, and redeem their credits. This is an initiative from the Ministry of Education, Government of India to enable academic flexibility and students’ mobility across multiple Higher Education Institutes (HEIs).  The University Grants Commission (UGC) has mandated that every learner who is an Indian national needs to submit the following details for creating their ABC account: 

  • Roll number issued by the university 
  • Aadhar number (12 digits) 
  • Name (as mentioned in Aadhaar) 
  • Gender 
  • Date of Birth 
  • Mobile number (linked to their Aadhaar) 

The university will reach out to enrolled students for details required to create their ABC accounts and will provide these details to UGC. Once the ABC account is created, the credits earned by the learners in their term-end exam will get deposited into their respective ABC accounts which they can redeem within 7 years. Students can email info@onlinemanipaladmission.in or call +91-8920 785 477 for any queries.

Who should opt for an Online BBA?

An Online BBA is ideal for learners who want to have a core understanding in business and management while having the benefit of flexible learning. It is preffered by fresh 12th-grade graduates, working professionals looking to upgrade their qualifications, aspiring entrepreneurs, and individuals who are looking forward to studying at their own pace while gaining industry-relevant skills.

More FAQs

When will I get access to the learning modules?

After admission is confirmed by the university, students will receive access to the Learning Management System (LMS), student portal and e-library. Students can then start accessing the course material and begin their learning journey.

What are the study materials given to learners of online degree programs at Sikkim Manipal University?

Students are given access to exhaustive e-library, video lectures, audio-visual interactive material, virtual classroom sessions, audio podcasts, virtual simulations, and self-assessment quizzes or tests. Students are free to download e-books and print them for convenience.

How can I get my academic queries answered?

Students will be assigned dedicated course mentors and coordinators who can be contacted to clarify doubts using discussion forums, instant messaging, and email on the Learning Management System (LMS).

What is the evaluation & assessment pattern? 

A student’s performance evaluation will be based on both continuous assessments (CA) through assignments and term-end examinations (TEE) which consist of descriptive and multiple-choice questions. A student must score at least 40% in each of these to be declared as having passed the exam. The assessment ratio of TEE to CA is 70:30. A student must score at least 40% in both the parts to be declared as having passed.

What is the examination pattern for online degrees at Sikkim Manipal University?

  • The University term-end examination (TEE) will be held digitally viz. computer-based and online proctored. 
  • Candidates can appear for the exams from the comfort of their homes. 
  • The details of the examination schedule will be made available in the photo admit card. Other details on the examination pattern are available in the program guide provided to students. 
  • Examination results will be published on the student portal. If a student fails to clear any of the papers (either in UE or IA or both), he must re-appear by applying to the University through the re-sitting application along with relevant fees, within the due date. 
  • On satisfying all criteria stipulated by the University, successful students will be awarded their respective program degree by the University.

How much time should I dedicate to complete the learning activities?

Although the learning is flexible, students are expected to spend at least 15-20 hours per week watching concept videos, reading learning material, attempting quizzes, and attending live webinars. This would ensure that you get your degree within the minimum duration.

What are the BBA course subjects?

The online BBA course covers basic subjects like Principles of Management, Business Economics, Financial Management, Business Communication, and Business Law, Business Finance. It additionally deals with specialized areas such as Fintech, Entrepreneurship, Operations, & Banking Insurance. The curriculum aims to give students a firm grasp of business ideas and at the same time, skills that are relevant to the industry.

More FAQs

What is the duration of the BBA program?

Like an on-campus BBA, the online BBA from Sikkim Manipal University is a three-year degree program consisting of six semesters. However, students can choose to complete the degree at their own pace in a maximum of 6 years.

How are Sikkim Manipal University’s online degree programs delivered? 

Sikkim Manipal University’s online programs are delivered in a 100% online mode so that students can attend live classes and complete the program remotely, while also pursuing their professional and personal commitments in parallel.

Will the program have live lectures and webinars?

Yes. The program is delivered through a mix of live lectures, pre-recorded videos and discussion forums in which learners can interact directly with faculty, and self-paced learning. Self-paced learning is driven by video lectures, e-books, quizzes, and assignments that are delivered using a state-of-the-art Learning Management System (LMS).

What specializations are available in the online BBA program at Sikkim Manipal University?

The online BBA from Sikkim Manipal University offers four specializations. Learners can choose from Business Analytics & Fintech, Entrepreneurship, Operations & Supply Chain Management, and Banking & Insurance.

What is the minimum grade to complete the online degree program?

Students must score at least 40% in continuous evaluation and 40% in term-end examinations in each course to successfully complete the program. In case the minimum score is not attained, they will need to apply for re-sit with applicable fees and appear for exams to pass the course.

How is an online BBA different from a regular BBA?

Here’s a quick comparison to help your understanding of the differences between online BBA courses and regular BBA programs:  

Aspect  Online BBA  Regular BBA 
Mode of Learning  Delivered through digital platforms with live and recorded sessions  Conducted through in-person classroom lectures 
Flexibility  Flexible schedule; study from anywhere  Fixed timetable with on-campus attendance 
Accessibility  Accessible from any location with internet access  Requires physical presence on campus 
Cost  Generally, more cost-effective; no relocation or commuting expenses  Higher overall cost including travel, accommodation, and campus fees 
Learning Environment  Virtual classrooms, discussion forums, digital collaboration  Face-to-face interaction, campus activities, peer engagement 
Suitability  Ideal for working professionals and self-paced learners  Ideal for students seeking traditional campus experience 

Does WES (World Education Services) evaluate online degrees from Sikkim Manipal University?

Sikkim Manipal University degrees issued after 2016 are evaluated by WES (World Education Services).
