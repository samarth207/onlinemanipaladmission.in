Explore the field of mathematical economics methods with an MSc in Mathematics with the focus of Econometrics. Why Choose Our Online MSc in Mathematics

Create Strong Foundations in the Subject

Establishing strong foundations in econometrics involves combining mathematically derived principles with statistical-based reasoning. With this program, you will develop the capabilities of formulating, estimating, and validating an economic model that can then be used for real world analysis.

Utilize Data-Driven Analysis of the Economy

Experiment with real world data through the utilization of Econometric techniques for economic, financial, and social investigations. The practical application of these tools, techniques, and the application of the econometrics will allow the individuals to develop skills in assessing data patterns, validating data trends, and using the assessments to make data driven decisions.

Have Mastery of Advanced Quantitative and Bayesian Methods

The use of advanced Econometric and Bayesian techniques will provide you with the ability to handle large, complicated datasets, with a high degree of uncertainty, and to use predictive modelling.

Get Prepared for Research and Academia

This program is designed to develop the student’s critical thinking skills and research potential necessary for achieving a higher level of education or an academic career.

    Learn More About Our MSc Program (FAQs)

    What are the eligibility criteria for this Online MSc in Mathematics course? Who can apply?

    The eligibility criteria for Manipal University Jaipur’s online MSc Mathematics are given below for non-Indian students.

    Candidates must have either a BSc degree or a 10+2+3/4 years equivalent Bachelor’s degree with Mathematics as a compulsory subject, from a University/Institution recognized by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) or any other competent body.

    Other requirements

    Candidates who are not Indian citizens or residing outside India and holding NRE or PIO status will be billed an international fee for respective degrees & may need to submit documents mentioned but not limited to CV, address proof, valid visa, PR card & passport copy as part of the application process.

    What is the duration of this MSc Mathematics program?

    Like an on-campus MSc Mathematics, the online MSc Mathematics from Manipal University Jaipur is a two-year degree program consisting of four semesters. However, students can choose to complete the degree at their own pace in a maximum of 4 years.

    What is the admission process for this online MSc Mathematics degree?

    Here’s a guide to help you begin your online MSc Mathematics program at Manipal University Jaipur: 

    • Step 1: Fill in your basic, education, & work experience-related details and pay the application fee to register. 
    • Step 2: Pay the admission fee for the first semester/year or full program. 
    • Step 3: Upload supporting documents and submit your application.  
    • Step 4: The university will evaluate your documents to confirm your admission. 

    Who should opt for online MSc Mathematics courses?

    Anyone who is looking to enhance their career, switch fields, or study while working can definitely consider it. It is a great option for people who are not able to go to physical classes because of lack of time or other commitments.

    What are the documents required for admission into this online MSc in Mathematics course?

    Mandatory documents: 

    • Proof of identity – Aadhaar card (mandatory for Indian citizens) 
    • Proof of address – Aadhaar card, passport, voter’s ID, gas bill, bank statement, post-paid mobile statement 
    • Class 10 & 12 marksheet 
    • Graduation marksheet (for PG students) 

    Non-mandatory documents: 

    • Work experience certificate

    • Scholarship documents 
    • Defense Service certificate

    Is work experience mandatory for this MSc Mathematics program? What qualifies as work experience?

    The online MSc Mathematics program at Manipal University Jaipur does not require work experience. Though it is always good to have work experience as it can provide context to the learning and also make classroom discussions more interesting.

    Is it mandatory for all online students to have an Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) account?

    Yes. From the academic year 2022-23, it has been mandatory for all universities offering courses in online or distance learning modes to create ABC accounts for all their students as per the UGC regulation and the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020. 

    Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) is a digital storehouse of the credits earned by students during their academic journey. It is a credit bank for students that allows them to accumulate, verify, transfer, and redeem their credit. This is an initiative from the Ministry of Education, Government of India to enable academic flexibility and students’ mobility across multiple Higher Education Institutes (HEIs).  The University Grants Commission (UGC) has mandated that every learner who is an Indian national need to submit the following details for creating their ABC account: 

    • Roll number issued by the university 
    • Aadhar number (12 digits) 
    • Name (as mentioned in Aadhaar) 
    • Gender 
    • Date of Birth 
    • Mobile number (linked to their Aadhaar) 

    The university will reach out to enrolled students for details required to create their ABC accounts and will provide these details to the UGC. Once the ABC account is created, the credits earned by the learners in their term-end exam will get deposited into their respective ABC accounts which they can redeem within 7 years. Students can email info@onlinemanipaladmission.in or call +91-8920 785 477 for any queries.

    Are flexible payment options available for these courses? What is the eligibility criteria?

    Yes, students can use numerous payment options. They can either pay the entire fees at one go or choose to pay it in annual, semester-wise installments, or if they are Indian students, they can avail the EMI facility without any cost through a bank that is financially partnered with the university.

    Are online MSc Mathematics classes more affordable than regular MSc Mathematics classes?

    Yes, they are more affordable because of lower tuition and no relocation or living expenses. With flexible payment options, they are more affordable.

    If I am eligible for a scholarship and pay the entire fee upfront, can I avail both the scholarship and the fee concession (for full payment)?

    Yes. All learners who pay the full program fee upfront can avail 10% fee concession on total program fee upon approval. In addition to the fee concession, if a learner is eligible for a scholarship, he/she can avail the same. The scholarship amount will be calculated on the total program fee.

    If I am funding my fee through a bank loan, can I apply for a fee concession for upfront payment of full program fee?

    • Fee concessions for upfront payment apply only when the program fee is paid by the learner through their own source of funds, such as their own bank account or card payment.
    • Learners who take an independent bank education loan (without subvention or zero-interest EMI) are eligible for this concession.
    • Learners opting for loans with subvention or zero-interest EMI schemes are not eligible for upfront payment concessions, but they may apply for scholarships if they meet the eligibility criteria.

    Can online degrees from Manipal University Jaipur be recognized in all regions of Canada?

    Yes, Manipal University Jaipur’s degrees are recognized in Canada. Here are the bodies that evaluates Manipal University Jaipur’s degrees for issuing equivalence certificates . These bodies conduct educational credential assessment (ECA) services for both employers and higher education institutions (HEIs).

    World Education Services (WES) – Applicable across US and Canada.
    International Credential Assessment Service of Canada (ICAS)- Applicable across Canada for employment and immigration purposes. ICAS is the only accepted evaluation service for admission to colleges in Ontario province.
    International Credential Evaluation Service (ICES)- The government-mandated ECA service in British Columbia which also serves the interest of all internationally educated applicants throughout Canada.
