The placement journey

With a systematic approach, Online Manipal prepares learners to be career-ready upon successful completion of their online programs.

Student Profile
Begin your placement journey by updating your profile on the portal. Fill in your personal details, academic qualifications & professional goals. A complete and well-maintained profile increases your chances of being shortlisted by recruiters.
Placement Orientation
Session for you to understand the entire placement process, the timelines, and the various steps involved before the placement cycle begins.
Upskilling
Elevate your career prospects by developing skills that employers value. Our 1700+ curated role-based & skill-focused learning modules focus on key areas across industries to help you build job-ready skills.
Verbal ability
Modules focused on improving core verbal abilities including grammar, reading comprehension, and communication. These improve professional communication, laying a strong foundation for interviews.
Employability Skills
Learning modules curated by industry experts cover resume building, professional etiquette, Group Discussions, Personal Interviews, LinkedIn Profile building, Job Scouting and behavioral skills.
Functional & Technical Skills
Role-specific modules aligned with your career goals such as Excel, Finance, Email writing, HR practices, and more.

*All the sessions are covered in videos (self-learning format) accessible to you at any time.

Placement Portal Walkthrough
Walkthrough of the AI-powered Online Manipal Placement Portal to learn about assessments, feedback, apply for jobs, track applications, and update documents.
Assessment
Identify your strengths & areas of improvement through preliminary skill assessment to increase the chances of securing a job of your interest.
Enrolment
Enrol on our AI-enabled Placement Portal to receive timely notifications about suitable job openings, apply for those jobs, and keep track of your applications.
Placement Drives
Regular job postings provide you with the opportunity to apply for jobs that align with your profile, interests, skillsets, and career goals. The placement portal opens a myriad of opportunities, from placement assistance to job fairs.

Frequently asked questions

What is 'Placement Assistance' offered by Online Manipal?

Online Manipal provides placement assistance throughout the duration of its online degree programs. Each semester is designed to strengthen learners’ job readiness through skill-based certification modules, curated self-paced learning content, and industry interaction sessions. This is followed by recruitment drives featuring opportunities across industries.

Do you organize job fairs? If yes, when?

Yes. Job fairs are organized based on market conditions and the requirements of our hiring partners. With retained past experiences from virtual job fairs, On June 13th, 2025, Online Manipal conducted Launchpad – India’s first in-person job fair for Online Degree Learners, at Bengaluru. Learners will be promptly informed of any upcoming job fairs, whether virtual or in-person.

Is placement assistance provided for learners with various levels of experience?

Yes. Placement assistance is provided to all learners, regardless of their experience levels. We assist learners by sharing relevant job opportunities. Learners are advised to keep their LinkedIn profiles updated and remain active on LinkedIn and job portals.

How will learners get information on placement process?

The Career Services team conducts placement orientation sessions at the start of a learner’s academic journey, followed by multiple live engagement sessions throughout the program. Key information is made available on the Learning Management System (LMS) and communicated via the learner’s university email. For any additional queries regarding placement assistance, learners may reach out to the help desk or connect with their academic counsellor.

Who is eligible for Placement Assistance?

All learners enrolled in our programs are eligible for placement assistance.

Do learners have to pay to participate in placement drives?

No. We provide Placement Assistance free of charge to our learners. However, any additional training required for professional, or skill development may be chargeable.

How long will I get placement support from Online Manipal?

Learners will receive comprehensive placement assistance throughout their academic journey, encompassing both upskilling and access to job opportunities. This support extends across the duration of the program and may continue for up to six months after the completion of the degree.

Do you assure a minimum number of interview chances?

We share interested learners’ profiles with prospective recruiters, and it is up to the companies to shortlist and invite shortlisted candidates for the subsequent interview processes. Learners may attend as many interviews as they are shortlisted for, until they are made an offer.

How many job offers can a student accept?

We maintain a ‘one student, one job offer’ policy. Once shortlisted, learners can appear for multiple interviews until they are made an offer.

Are learners provided with internship opportunities?

Yes. Career Services collaborates with organizations and leading career opportunity providers to secure internships for our learners. Many companies offer “intern to hire” positions, creating pathways to full-time employment. However, the final selection of candidates is determined by hiring organizations based on their recruitment policies, requirements, and candidate suitability.

Do you have tie-ups with HR consultancies?

Yes. We maintain a healthy relationship with several consulting firms, and they share their requirements with us on a regular basis.

Can learners switch job domains upon completion of the program?

Yes, it is possible. Such job seekers must work on projects that show relevance and interest in their preferred domains. Also, they must attain pertinent certifications to strengthen their profile. Learners are encouraged to apply to positions across domains and industries basis their interests and relevant skillsets.

Is there any lock-in period/ minimum period for which a student must work with a company after getting placed?

This depends on the position and nature of work and is at the discretion of the recruiting company.

Will I get a job with a work-from-home option, or do I need to go to the office for work?

The specific requirements for a job vary depending on the position and nature of the work. It’s advisable to check the requirements of each company before applying.

Do you assure placements or provide placement guarantee?

No. We do not give placement assurance or guarantee. However, all eligible learners will receive placement assistance.

Will attending the placement training sessions add to my attendance requirement for the program?

No. Career advisory and skill development programs are additional benefits provided to our learners. This does not contribute towards the attendance requirement for the program.
