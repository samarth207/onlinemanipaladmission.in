The online Postgraduate Diploma in Entrepreneurship & Innovation (PGD-E&I) from Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) is designed to mirror all key aspects of a learner's entrepreneurial journey. This entrepreneurship diploma aims to provide in-depth understanding of core concepts for aspiring entrepreneurs, early-stage start-up founders, and successors of family businesses. This outcome-focused entrepreneurship program is delivered entirely by seasoned industry leaders through live interactive classes and on-demand recorded content, enriched with case studies, practical business insights, and mentorship from industry experts. Learners can also receive exclusive access to an integrated startup ecosystem from MILES (Manipal Centre for Innovation Leadership and Entrepreneurship), including the incubator platforms at Manipal Academy of Higher Education, guidance on product development, go-to-market strategy, preparation of pitch documentation and approach to grants and funding strategies.

    Semester 1

    Business Strategy & MOAT

    Learn how businesses analyse industries, build advantage, and sustain growth. This course introduces powerful strategy frameworks, competitive positioning, and Blue Ocean thinking, while integrating sustainability and long-term value creation. Learners build the ability to translate strategic insight into actionable business decisions.

    Creating a Strong Business Model

    Turn ideas into viable ventures. Learn how to create a solid business plan, understand customer needs, develop go-to-market strategies, and build a compelling pitch deck. Alongside business modelling, learners also develop essential financial awareness through key metrics that support long-term decision-making.

    Entrepreneurial Mindset and Journey

    Develop the mindset of an entrepreneur. This course strengthens creativity, opportunity recognition, adaptability, and decision-making under uncertainty. It also introduces the stages of the entrepreneurial journey from ideation and product development to funding, scale, and long-term growth.

    Sales, Marketing, and Branding

    Master how businesses attract, convert, and retain customers. Learn the fundamentals of selling, pricing, positioning, digital marketing, branding, and communication. This course prepares learners to engage customers effectively and build brand credibility across markets.

    Problem Statement and Trends

    Learn to identify meaningful problems and understand how global and industry trends shape opportunities. The course strengthens customer understanding, decision-making, and pattern recognition while developing the ability to design future-ready solutions.

    Project 1

    At the end of Semester 1, learners work on a capstone project where they present their own entrepreneurial idea. They apply core concepts from the semester to clearly define a problem, analyse market and trend insights, and propose a practical, real-world solution.
    Semester 2

    Financial Acumen for Entrepreneurs

    Build financial confidence through business metrics, financial statements, unit economics, and investment indicators. This course enables data-driven decision-making and prepares learners for sustainable growth and investor conversations.

    Corporate Governance, Risk and Compliance

    Understand how ethical conduct, risk management, and compliance shape resilient organisations. Learn about governance practices, regulatory requirements, and the role of technology in building trustworthy and sustainable enterprises.

    Investors’ Expectations & Fund Raising

    Learn what investors look for and how funding decisions are made. Explore funding stages, investor categories, pitch evaluation, and common fund-raising pitfalls while strengthening venture readiness for capital.

    Structuring and Scaling Your Organization

    Understand how organisations scale through people, culture, systems, and leadership. Learn how HR becomes a strategic engine for performance, governance, and long-term growth.

    Identification of Right Investors and Valuation

    Learn how to select the right investors and understand how valuation works at different business stages. Develop the ability to assess investor fit, negotiate terms, and build strong, sustainable long-term funding partnerships.

    Project 2

    At the end of Semester 2, learners take the same idea forward and bring it to completion. They refine their concept into an investment-ready venture and gain the opportunity to present it to investors, incubators, and key stakeholders.

    Frequently asked questions

    What is the eligibility criteria for online Entrepreneurship & Innovation diploma course? Who can apply?

    Educational Qualification 

    Candidates must have completed 10+2+3-year bachelor’s degree from a recognized university/institution, or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities.

    Grades 

    Candidates must have minimum 50% marks in aggregate or equivalent grade in graduation.

    What is the duration of  this Entrepreneurship & Innovation program?

    Like an on-campus PGD in Entrepreneurship & Innovation program, the online program is also a one-year diploma consisting of two semesters.

    What is the admission process for this online Entrepreneurship & Innovation diploma course?

    Here’s a guide to help you begin your online Entrepreneurship & Innovation diploma at Manipal Academy of Higher Education:

    • Step 1: Fill in your basic, education, and work experience-related details and pay the application fee to register.
    • Step 2: Pay the admission fee for the first semester/year or full program.
    • Step 3: Upload supporting documents and submit your application.
    • Step 4: The university will evaluate your documents to confirm your admission.

    Why choose this Entrepreneurship & Innovation PG diploma course from Online Manipal?

    • UGC-entitled: MAHE is approved under the University Grants Commission (UGC) entitled universities and authorized to conduct online programs.
    • NAAC A++ Accredited University: MAHE is a NAAC A++ accredited university, which guarantees the quality of education and the credibility of the institution.
    • Expert Faculty & Industry-Focused Curriculum: The course content is planned and taught by experts. Also, the curriculum is revised periodically to comply with industry standards.
    • 100% Online with Flexible Scheduling: High quality anytime and anywhere learning is ideal for working professionals or graduates who want to learn new skills without interrupting their career.
    • Chance to incubate at Manipal’s Tech & Bio Incubator platform.
    • Access to MILES ecosystem: Testing facilities, Idea café, 3D printers, an Apple creative studio, and more.
    • Guidance on government grants like Nidhi EIR, Prayaas & SSP, BIRAC-BIG, and more.
    • Opportunity to showcase business ideas with Seed investors like 3one4 Capital, Ventureast, and Speciale Invest.
    • Case studies on driving innovation & gaining deeper understanding of product philosophy.

    Is this Entrepreneurship & Innovation course for freshers or experienced professionals?

    Anyone who is looking to enhance their career, switch fields, or study while working can definitely consider it. It is a great option for people who are not able to go to physical classes because of lack of time or other commitments.

    *What are the terms & conditions for EMIs and other easy financing options?

    *EMI amount mentioned above is applicable for the entire program fee and would need to be paid monthly, for the full duration of the program.

    Approval of loans from our loan partners depend on applicants meeting credit norms and submitting required documents. Loan partners may impose other fees and may have additional terms & conditions that must be met by applicants.

    For further details on EMIs and other easy financing options, please contact our admission counsellors.

    How can I access the learning materials for this course?

    Every study resource such as videos, quizzes, PDFs, and recorded lectures can be accessed at any time through the Online Manipal’s Learning Management System (LMS).

    What are the subjects in this Entrepreneurship & Innovation diploma course?

    The online Entrepreneurship & Innovation certification consists of various core subjects like Business Strategy & MOAT, Sales, Marketing, and Branding, Creating a Strong Business Model, Financial Acumen for Entrepreneurs, Corporate Governance, Risk and Compliance, Investors’ Expectations & Fund Raising, and Structuring and Scaling Your Organization.
