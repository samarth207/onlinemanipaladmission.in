Eligibility Criteria for Online BCom

Educational qualification

Candidates must have completed 10+2 from a recognized national or state board institution or 10+3 diploma from a recognized national or state institute.

Grades

Candidates must have at least 40% marks in aggregate in 10+2 / diploma (35% for reserved categories).

Educational Qualification

Candidates must have completed 10+2/ intermediate/ senior secondary education from a recognized board, or 10+3 diploma from a recognized national or state institute, or an equivalent qualification as recognized by the Association of Indian Universities, in any discipline.

Online BCom Projects

Projects are essential to the overall program and provide opportunities for students to use theoretical concepts within an actual business situation. Projects allow students to build their analytical thoughts and develop solutions to problems. This table shows the major objectives of the project and the specific structure for selection and evaluation. The goal of projects is to have students able to apply what they have learned in class to actual problems. This will develop their ability to conduct independent research and solve multidisciplinary problems. In determining project topics, students will work with faculty to ensure their topics are applicable to industry trends as well as their academic areas of interest. Faculty provide continuous advice and support as students complete their projects. Final evaluations are based on pre-defined criteria for academic and practical assessment.

Project Objective Project Selection & Evaluation Process
1. Develop the ability to apply multi-disciplinary concepts.
1. Topic selection is done in consultation with the faculty guide and approved by the institution.
2. The student submits the final project report in the prescribed format.
3. Evaluation is based on the quality of research work, analysis, and presentation.
4. The final assessment includes a viva voce, and marks are awarded based on the project report and viva performance.
2. Practice the tools and techniques to solve organizational problems.

How to choose the right BCom specialization for your career?

You will want to start by identifying your personal characteristics, strengths and weaknesses; this includes interests related to data and accounting, and you may want to therefore look towards a career in accounting or finance. The accounting and tax industries are consistently projected to sustain demand for years; this is particularly true due to the changing nature of taxation and other related laws governing goods and services tax (GST). Finance professionals are needed in both banks and corporate treasuries. 

After you’ve made an assessment of your personal strengths and market needs, take a look at your current position. If you’re in retail or operations, a marketing or e-commerce specialization complements your experience. For government job aspirants, Accounting with Taxation provides competitive advantages in banking exams. Another excellent resource for research is the alumni from your potential field of study; they have been down this road before and can provide you with great insights. Choose colleges that include fintech, data analytics, and international business in their curriculum to match current industry trends. Lastly, be sure to choose an area of specialization where the faculty are highly respected within the industry.

How does online BCom offer more flexibility than regular BCom classes?

The benefit of an online BCom is that you have the freedom to study from anywhere in the world! The lectures are live and recorded – you can watch them at your own pace and go back and watch them repeatedly until you fully understand the material. You can also arrange your study schedule around your employment and family commitments, which may be a challenge for many employed individuals who take classes on campus. If you are taking an online BCom program, you will not have a daily commute, save your valuable time, and reduce your expenses. Many online BCom programs hold live classes at different times, allowing students in different time zones to participate. The majority or all assignments will likely have flexible due dates.

Is online BCom valid in India?

Yes. An online BCom in India is provided by a university recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC). UGC has accepted distance and online learning degrees as equal to regular degrees. A degree course from UGC recognized institutions is valid for employers, government agencies, and for admission into higher degree courses. Therefore, confirm that the institution you select is UGC approved and NAAC- accredited. Before you apply, please confirm your institution with the list of accredited institutions.

What factors to consider before choosing an online BCom program?

  • Ensure the program is accredited, as only recognized degrees are valued by employers and higher education institutions. 
  • Verify that the curriculum includes updated subjects such as GST, digital finance, data analytics, and fintech, along with core commerce topics. 
  • Check whether the available specializations align with your long-term career goals. 
  • Review the academic qualifications and industry experience of the faculty members. 
  • Evaluate the university’s placement support services and the strength of its alumni network. 
  • Prefer institutions with dedicated placement cells for better internship and job opportunities. 
  • Confirm that the fee structure is transparent, including tuition and examination fees, with no hidden charges. 
  • Understand the examination pattern, whether online proctored or conducted at physical centers. 
  • Review the weightage of assignments and final exams in overall evaluation. 
  • Read reviews from current students and alumni to gain insights into program quality and outcomes.

How long does it take to get a BCom online?

Like an on-campus degree, the online BCom program is 3 years (36 months) in duration and can be completed within a maximum period of 6 years.

How much does online BCom cost?

The cost of online BCom programs varies significantly across institutions. Based on current offerings, fees typically range from approximately ₹75,000 to ₹3,00,000 for the complete three-year program.

How hard is an online BCom?

Individual interests determine the level of course difficulty for each person. Those with an affinity for accounting, tax preparation, and numbers tend to feel the courses are easy to complete and stimulating; however, do not believe that because it is offered online it will be easier than if you were attending an offline university. All UGC-accredited universities will provide a rigorous academic program, as well as the same level of academic expectations and exams as you would find in a standard university.

Will an online BCom help my career?

Completing an Online BCom degree can enhance your ability to find a job because specializations like Accounting, Finance, Taxation, Business Law and Management are of high demand in many professions. After graduation, students may find employment in their respective fields or continue with higher levels of study, i.e. MBA, MCom, CA, that will give them more opportunity for career advancement.

What are the eligibility criteria for online BCom?

The criteria for application for the online BCom program are: the candidate must have either a 10+2 from an accredited national/state board institution; or a 10+3 diploma from a nationally/state recognized institution.

What is the average salary after completing an online BCom course?

Across various roles, the approximate salary range for BCom graduates spans about INR 4 lakh to INR 18 lakh per annum, depending on the job role, experience, and specialization.

Which is the best institute for an online BCom?

It really depends on the institution’s reputation, curriculum, faculty, and placement opportunities.  Some of the best institutes as per students’ reviews that fulfill these criteria are Manipal University Jaipur, Sikkim Manipal University, Manipal Academy of Higher Education, and others.

Do I need to take exams to clear my online BCom?

Yes. There will be periodic assessments and semester-end examinations which students must attend for the completion of the online BCom course.
