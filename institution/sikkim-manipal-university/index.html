Sikkim Manipal University

Sikkim Manipal University (SMU), established in 1995, is an NAAC A+ accredited institution offering UGC-entitled degrees. With a strong legacy of academic excellence, SMU leverages state-of-the-art technology to deliver accessible, industry-relevant programs, ensuring students gain quality education.

SMU’s distance education arm, founded in 2001, has educated over 500,000 students, becoming a top provider of professional programs in India. Recognized for its excellence, including the ‘Most Preferred Distance Education University Among Students’ award, SMU now delivers affordable online degree programs in India, further extending its commitment to accessible education.
