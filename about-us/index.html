About Online Manipal

Online Manipal is the digital home of Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ), and Sikkim Manipal University (SMU). These three Manipal universities, accredited with NAAC A++ and NAAC A+, offer UGC-entitled online degrees equivalent to on-campus programs.

Since its inception in 2021, Online Manipal has empowered over 100,000 learners to realize their potential by providing them with accessible, affordable & industry-relevant degrees and new-age certifications in a 100% online mode.

Building a legacy of academic excellence

Dr. T.M.A. Pai laid the foundation of the Manipal Group in 1953 with the establishment of Kasturba Medical College in Manipal, Karnataka. This was just a stepping stone that paved the way for several other leading educational institutions that rewrote the legacy of academic excellence.

The Manipal Group has been benchmark of excellence in higher education shaping the academic landscape with universities like Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) & Sikkim Manipal University (SMU). This includes top-ranked institutes such as Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT), Kasturba Medical College (KMC), and T. A. Pai Management Institute (TAPMI). These educational institutions have created and nurtured bright minds who are now driving sustainable real-world impacts across the globe.
