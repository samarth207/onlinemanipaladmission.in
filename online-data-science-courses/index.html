Advantages of Our Data Science Courses

Comprehensive Curriculum

The data science courses are designed to provide a detailed study plan that encompasses the full scope of the data science lifecycle, such as data collection, analysis, machine learning, and visualization.

Flexible and Accessible Format

Offered in its entirety online, the data science master’s programs course allows learners to follow a self-paced learning model remotely. This flexibility suits working professionals or students who manage multiple responsibilities.

Hands-on Experience with Tools and Projects

Students can get involved in practical projects using Python, R, SQL, and Tableau tools. This real-world exposure fortifies conceptual understanding and supports shaping a portfolio highlighting job-ready skills.

Learn from Qualified Faculty

The online master’s degree in data science is taught by exceptionally qualified faculty members with academic knowledge and on-the-ground industry experience. Their supervision ensures learners obtain robust theoretical understanding and practical insights pertinent to today’s data-driven roles.

Manipal Alumni Status

Following completion, students gain alumnus status from either of the Manipal universities, engaging in a global network of professionals across different sectors. This interaction extends long-term value through networking opportunities, events, and career support.

    What is Data Science?

    Data Science is a multidisciplinary field that utilizes scientific methods, algorithms, and systems to pull out observations and understandings from structured and unstructured data. It brings together elements of statistics, computer science, and domain competence to lead strategic planning and advancement. 

    What does a Data Scientist do?

    A Data Scientist evaluates large volumes of data to disclose patterns, trends, and actionable insights. They work around predictive models, conduct statistical analysis, and employ machine learning to decipher complex problems and lead decision-making in business, healthcare, finance, and other sectors. 

    What are the top skills to become a Data Scientist?

    Key skills to becoming a Data Scientist include:  

    • Expertise in programming (Python, R) 
    • Insights on statistics and probability 
    • Data wrangling and cleaning 
    • Machine learning techniques 
    • Data visualization (using tools like Tableau or Power BI) 
    • Robust analytical and problem-solving skills 
    • Communication and storytelling with data
