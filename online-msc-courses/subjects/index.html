Online MSc Degree Syllabus & Subjects

Having acquainted with an online MSc helps learners to comprehend more on their theoretical knowledge alongside practical and industry-relevant skills. With various specializations to choose from, it provides niche expertise in Data Science, Business Analytics and fields of your choice. Students can pursue their MSc degree programs from Manipal Academy of Education (MAHE) to help them prepare roles in research, industry and academia.

Some of the subjects that learners come across during the MSc courses are Computational Mathematics, Machine Learning Methods, Research Methodology, Big Data Analytics, Time Series Analysis, Managerial Economics, and so on. The curriculum emphasizes analytical thinking, research aptitude, and application-based learning through projects, assignments, and virtual labs, preparing learners for roles in research, industry, and academia.
