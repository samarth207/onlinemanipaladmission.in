Frequently asked questions

What is Online Manipal?

Online Manipal is an online learning platform by the Manipal Group, offering online degree and certification programs from Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ), Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), and Sikkim Manipal University (SMU). These UGC-entitled online degree programs combine academic excellence with the flexibility of online delivery, thus enabling students from India and other countries to access quality education anytime, anywhere.

Are online degrees at Online Manipal UGC approved?

Yes, the online degree programs offered by Manipal universities via Online Manipal are entitled by the University Grants Commission (UGC), the premier higher education regulatory body in India which meets the global standards.

How is an online degree program different from a distance education program?

Online degree programs are delivered through an online learning portal, i.e., the Learning Management System (LMS). It supports anytime, anywhere learning using laptops, smartphones, or tablets, with exams also being conducted remotely. With the LMS, learners can access a range of learning resources, including video tutorials, live interactive classes, recorded sessions, discussion forums, quizzes, and exams.
On the contrary, distance education primarily relies on self-paced learning with very little interaction. The learning materials are provided physically, and students need to visit the study centers frequently for academic support, contact classes, and examinations.

Are online degree programs from Manipal universities recognized and valid in India?

Online degree programs offered by Manipal universities via the Online Manipal platform are recognized and valid throughout India. These programs are UGC-entitled and approved by the UGC-DEB (University Grants Commission – Distance Education Bureau), which is the accreditation body for distance and online education in India. The degrees are offered by institutions such as Manipal Academy of Higher Education, Manipal University Jaipur, and Sikkim Manipal University, which are NAAC A++ and NAAC A+ accredited. These degrees are equivalent to on-campus degrees and are accepted for higher education and careers in both private and public sectors.

What are the benefits of pursuing online degree programs from Manipal universities?

Online Manipal is a platform that offers degree programs from Manipal universities. These courses are designed to meet the needs of working professionals and learners who require flexibility in learning, providing them with a versatile range of academic and career benefits. ~ Access to Coursera and other Global Platforms

~ Career Support Services

~ Campus Immersions

Do employers & recruiters accept online degrees?

Yes, employers & recruiters recognize and accept UGC-entitled online degrees from accredited universities, especially when they are industry-relevant and skill-focused.

Are online degrees offered at Online Manipal equivalent to on-campus degrees?

Yes. As per the UGC regulations, online degrees are equivalent to the corresponding degrees offered through conventional mode. Whether it is for a job, a promotion, or further studies, an online degree from any of the universities on Online Manipal is accepted with the same value as a traditional on-campus one. Moreover, online programs from Manipal universities are counterparts of their on-campus programs in terms of syllabus and academic credits.

Are online programs from Manipal universities globally recognized?

Yes, online degree programs from Manipal universities are backed by accreditations and assessments from respected international credential evaluation bodies such as World Education Services (WES), International Qualifications Assessment Service (IQAS), and International Credential Assessment Service of Canada (ICAS). In addition, Manipal institutions consistently feature in prestigious global rankings like the QS World University Rankings—affirming academic excellence and international credibility.

Are international students eligible to apply for programs at Online Manipal?

Yes. Online Manipal accepts applications from both Indian and international students for all programs. Non-Indian applicants should apply via www.onlinemanipaladmission.in/global
