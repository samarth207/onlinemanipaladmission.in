Step into the World of Financial Markets with Bloomberg at MUJ

This campus immersion program aims to equip online learners with a deep understanding of the functions of the global financial market using Bloomberg Terminal. Learners can earn top-rated industry certifications from Bloomberg through this 6-day intensive, on-campus experience.

Program Overview

Bloomberg Market Concepts (BMC) introduces the core ideas of global financial markets using Bloomberg’s real-time data and tools.

Delivered through state-of-the-art Bloomberg Terminal 

8 in-depth modules + real-world case studies

Recognized by top employers worldwide

Modules Covered 

Economic Indicators

Fixed Income

Terminal Basics

Stock Options

Currencies

Equities

Commodities  

Portfolio Management

What You’ll Learn? 

During the 6-day residential program, you will learn:

  • How to analyze and interpret macroeconomic data
  • Central bank strategies to control inflation and deflation 
  • Bond valuation through interest rate dynamics 
  • Terminal navigation & market data discovery using real tools

Eligibility & Delivery

  • Online students of Manipal University Jaipur
  • Open for only 240 learners on a first-come, first-served basis
  • Theory + 10-hour Bloomberg Terminal training
  • Learners are advised to bring their laptops for better utilization of time
